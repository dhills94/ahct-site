Here are some questions we usually get about Health Coverage Insurance

How Do I Buy Health Insurance Coverage?

Buying health insurance coverage starts with a health insurance quote. You can receive as many quotes as you'd like to compare plans and pricing from multiple carriers. Make sure you have all your personal information available when speaking with a licensed health insurance agent. Ask questions like "Can I keep my same doctors?" to find out what enrolling in a new health insurance plan will feel like.

How Much Will My Health Insurance Plan Cost?

The cost of your health insurance plan will vary depending on which state you live in, your age, gender, annual household income and the options you choose. These options include your health insurance copay, health insurance plan deductible, and health insurance plan network.

You can reduce your monthly health insurance premium by choosing a higher deductible, but in the event you need medical care, you will need to pay more up front before your health insurance plan begins to pay for your treatment. Therefore, while a high deductible may save you money every month in the short term, you risk paying a significant amount at one time in the event of a significant medical procedure. Consult with our team of experienced licensed agents and let us find you the best health insurance plan for the best price.

Can I Keep My Same Doctor When I Enroll In A New Health Insurance Plan?

If you're looking for a new health policy or to renew an existing plan, there are many options that will allow you to keep your same doctor. Not all providers are supported by all doctors, so you should contact one of our experienced health insurance agents to discuss your options if you're looking to maintain your current doctor and network.

Will My Prescriptions Be Covered?

Health insurance plans have varying levels of coverage and inclusions like remote doctor 24/7 access, preventative care coverage, and even prescription coverage. Many times, prescription coverage is included, or can be added on to your plan. If you need assistance or simply want to save money on your prescriptions, contact us today.
